# Retail Performance Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyses retail sales data using Microsoft Excel, MySQL and Microsoft Power BI to identify trends in sales, profitability, customer behaviour and regional performance. The project demonstrates an end-to-end business intelligence workflow, transforming transactional retail data into meaningful business insights and strategic recommendations.

The analysis answers key business questions using SQL before presenting the findings through interactive Power BI dashboards designed to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Project Objectives

The objectives of this project were to:

- Analyse sales and profit performance over time.
- Identify the most profitable product categories, sub-categories and products.
- Evaluate regional profitability and identify underperforming regions.
- Analyse customer segments and identify high-value customers.
- Investigate return rates across regions.
- Develop interactive Power BI dashboards to communicate business insights.
- Provide evidence-based recommendations to improve business performance.

## Key Business Insights

The analysis identified several important business insights:

- Sales and profit increased consistently between 2023 and 2026, indicating sustained business growth.
- Technology generated the highest total sales ($839,893.28) and total profit ($146,543.38).
- The Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier generated the highest individual product profit ($25,199.93).
- The Consumer segment contributed 46.66% of total profit, making it the organisation's most valuable customer segment.
- The West region generated the highest overall profit despite recording the highest return rate (11.56%).
- The Tables, Bookcases and Supplies sub-categories recorded overall losses, highlighting opportunities to improve profitability.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations were developed:

- Increase investment in high-performing Technology products.
- Review pricing and discount strategies for loss-making sub-categories.
- Investigate the causes of product returns in the West region.
- Apply successful regional strategies across lower-performing regions.
- Strengthen customer retention within the Consumer customer segment.
